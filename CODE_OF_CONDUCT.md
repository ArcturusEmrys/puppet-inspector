Those who wish to contribute to my grimoire are encouraged to familiarize themselves with the [Rust Code of Conduct](https://rust-lang.org/policies/code-of-conduct/).

In addition, the Arcturus Emrys community rules also apply:

1. Conduct unbecoming of a scholar of the arcane arts is forbidden. This includes bullying, harassment, and so on, either here or in the mundane world.

2. The mundane name of any inhabitant of the magical world is forbidden knowledge and shall not be disseminated in this grimoire.

3. It is prohibited to post the babblings of magical constructs, in the form of text or image, as if it were your own creation. Posting such "AI Art" is highly discouraged and may be removed as spam.
